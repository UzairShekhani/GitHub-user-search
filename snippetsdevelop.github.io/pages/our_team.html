Create Our Team Section using HTML CSS

The "Our Team" section on a webpage plays a crucial role in humanizing the brand and establishing a sense of trust with the audience. It provides visitors with a glimpse into the faces and personalities behind the company, fostering a deeper connection and making the brand more relatable. This section showcases the skills and expertise of team members, instilling confidence in potential customers or clients.
